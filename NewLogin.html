Normal Hours of Operation are from 7:30 AM until 3:00 AM Daily. Please click 'Login to HokieSPA,' then enter your PID and password and click 'Login.' Follow the prompts to enroll in 2-Factor Authentication, or to authenticate using your second factor. By logging in with your PID and password you agree to abide by the terms and conditions of the Virginia Tech Policy: Acceptable Use of Information Systems

If you need to create a PID, please use the PID Generation Tool

Once logged in, you will see a menu corresponding to your role as a student, faculty, employee or alumnus.

When you are finished, please Exit and close your browser to protect your privacy.
